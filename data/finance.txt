Mutual funds are long-term investment options suitable for wealth creation.

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) helps in disciplined investing.

Emergency fund should be equal to 3 to 6 months of expenses.

Diversification helps reduce investment risk.

High rent exceeding 40 percent of income is considered financially risky.

Spending less than 10 percent on food may indicate poor budgeting or unhealthy lifestyle.

Saving at least 20 percent of income is recommended.

Budgeting rules:
- Track expenses regularly
- Reduce unnecessary spending
- Follow 50/30/20 rule

Expense control:
- Identify needs vs wants
- Cut non-essential costs
